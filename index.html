About The Project

Our Journey

BarberFactory was born from a frustrating experience we both shared. One busy weekend, Khaled spent hours calling different barbershops, only to find them fully booked or unreachable. Meanwhile, Khairi had to wait for nearly two hours at his local barbershop due to an inefficient walk-in system.

This common problem sparked an idea: why not create a solution that benefits both clients and barbers? As software engineering students at Holberton School, we saw an opportunity to transform this challenge into our Portfolio Project.

Over eight intensive weeks, we developed BarberFactory from concept to completion. We conducted interviews with local barbers and clients, understanding their pain points and needs.
